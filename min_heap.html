Key Properties
🔺
Min at root: The smallest element is always at index 0. Here it's 1.
📐
Parent ≤ Children: Every parent is smaller than or equal to both its children. e.g. 3 ≤ 7 and 3 ≤ 9.
🌳
Complete Binary Tree: All levels are fully filled except possibly the last, filled left to right.
⚡
Time complexity: Insert O(log n) · Extract min O(log n) · Peek min O(1)
🐍
Python usage: import heapq — heapq.heappush(h, val) · heapq.heappop(h)
